About Victoria Hwang

A San José State University Art Student studying in Digital Media art. A current focus on getting the core fundamentals of what it means being a digital artist.

Artist Statement: The reason I choose to be an artist is not because I just enjoy it, but I live and thrive in such works of effort and passion. I want to make an impact on someones life, have enjoyment and fufillment in what I and many people strive to create. I am of many artists, coders, and definitly a speck to the amazing world of gamers, but each person makes their own unique contribution, and that is something I find special and motivating.
